We perform everything in construction and painting, large and small.
We do concrete work, basic insulation, alterations and extensions, roof and facade work,
kitchen and bathroom renovations, painting work inside and outside, etc. Our customers are industries
and real estate companies as well as private individuals and municipalities. We work mainly in Helsingborg.
We work with selected subcontractors in, for example, electricity, plumbing, excavation, masonry and tiling.
